Liz Abinante is an engineer living in Portland working for New Relic. She has served as co-leader for the Chicago chapter of Girl Develop It, and currently volunteers as an advisor and for the curriculum review team nationally. She is infectiously enthusiastic about web development, teaching, learning, and feminism. Her greatest dream in life is to spend her entire day surrounded by puppies while coding.
