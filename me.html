Hello!

 

My name is Liz Abinante* and I'm 27. I'm from California, currently living in Chicago. I'm very crafty, okay with HTML, lovely at CSS, and learning Ruby.

I'm a strong, ambitious woman, with numerous hobbies and passions. Some of these include:

  • Knitting (which I formed a small business doing)
  • Biking and jogging (I ran my first 5k the day after my 26th birthday)
  • Home made local, whole food
  • Tina Fey and other trail blazing women in comedy
  • Reading (trashy novels, and good stuff too)
  • Wine (and cheese, but that's a given)

I spent many years during my younger days supporting and campaigning for women's equality, gay marriage, and feminism. I also hold a Master of Arts degree in Women and Gender Studies, in addition to a dual Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology and Women's Studies. I wrote a Master's thesis called Bite Me, I'm Yours: Female Readership, Vampire Romance, and Negotiating Ideology.

I moved to Chicago for a job in 2011 and now own a home in the city. Someday, I'd like to move back to California.

As a woman entering the tech world, I'm hoping to grow gender equality in the field by encouraging young women to learn to code. I've got lots of ideas about how to do this, and I hope to implement them soon.
